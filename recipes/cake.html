Isabele's Cake

Cake created yesterday by Isabele

The recipe is fit to be eaten cold, as a dessert.

Should be enough for 4 to 6 people.

  • 500g of flour
  • 500g of milk
  • half a cup of tea of yeast
  • 300g of Nestle Milk
  • 500g of Milk Cream
  • 3 lemons
  • 3 eggs
  1. Add flour, Milk and yeast to a bowl and mix them until it becomes homogeneous
  2. Leave it to rest for a couple of minutes
  3. Get the Nestle Milk and Milk Cream mix them, then add the lemons's juice to it.
  4. Get the eggs's white and boil it until the smell comes out
  5. Mix the eggs's white with the Nestle Milk's mix
  6. Bake the dough
  7. Add the Nestle Milk's mix to the top of the dough
  8. Leave it to rest on the fridge for a couple minutes and its finished.
