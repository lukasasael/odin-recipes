Isabele's Cake

Cake created yesterday by Isabele

The recipe is fit to be eaten cold, as a dessert.

Should be enough for 4 to 6 people.

Ingredients:

  • 2 Tea-cups of flour
  • 4 Spoons of Butter
  • half a cup of tea of yeast
  • 300g of Condensed milk
  • Half a can of Milk Cream
  • 3 lemons
  • 3 eggs's white
  • half a tea-spoon of sugar

Step by Step:

  1. Add flour, 150g of Milk Cream, 4 spoons of butter and a spoon of yeast to a bowl and mix them until it becomes homogeneous
  2. Leave it to rest for a couple of minutes
  3. Get the Condensed milk and Milk Cream mix them, then add the lemons's juice to it.
  4. Get the eggs's white and boil it until the smell comes out
  5. Mix the eggs's white with the Condensed milk mix
  6. Bake the dough
  7. Add the Condensed milk mix to the top of the dough
  8. Leave it to rest on the fridge for a couple minutes and its finished.
